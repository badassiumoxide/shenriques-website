Sandra Henriques Gajjar was born in the Azores in 1978 and has been based in Lisbon since 1997. She blogs about cultural tourism at Tripper, is a contributing writer for Lonely Planet and has worked as a freelance ghostwriter and copywriter in a variety of projects since 2014. She is also the author of Lisbon Travel Guide for Urban Explorers.

Sandra is a detail-oriented writer who specializes in writing high-quality, SEO optimized content for the web, particularly in the travel niche.
